About Me


I am a tech-savvy teen who likes to create software. I started getting interested in computers when I was 10. I first learned how to use a

computer then, and I became very interested in using computers. From then on, I used Scratch and Turbowarp to create web-based software,

such as weather monitors, a few games, and multiple failed attempts at making a simple AI model. When I was 13, I picked up the belief of

FOSS software. I switched most of my computers to Linux, and now I make and use lots of free software.
